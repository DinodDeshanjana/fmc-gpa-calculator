GPA Calculation Method

GPA is the credit-weighted arithmetic mean of all the Grade Points (GP) obtained by a student for the course units he/she offered for a particular academic year. This will be calculated to the second decimal place according to the following formula.

Official Formula

GPA = Σ
(Credit × Grade Point) Total Credits
Note on Auxiliary Courses: Subjects such as Sinhala Language or Tamil Language may not be counted toward class awarding. However, students must obtain at least a minimum pass in these courses to complete the degree.
