A simple and user-friendly **web-based GPA calculator** developed for students of the **Faculty of Management and Commerce (FMC)** at **South Eastern University of Sri Lanka (SEUSL)**.

This tool helps students easily calculate their **Semester GPA** and **Cumulative Degree GPA**, allowing them to track their academic progress and verify their **degree eligibility** according to FMC academic regulations.

## 🧮 GPA Calculation Formula

The GPA is calculated using the **credit-weighted arithmetic mean** of grade points.

GPA = Σ (Credit Hours × Grade Point) / Σ Credit Hours

Where:

• **Credit Hours** = Number of credits assigned to a course unit

• **Grade Point** = Grade value assigned according to the FMC grading scheme

Example:

((A × 3 credits) + (B+ × 3 credits) + (A- × 2 credits)) / Total Credits Attempted

## 📚 FMC Grading System

Based on the Faculty of Management and Commerce Undergraduate Guide (SEUSL), Academic Year 2021/2022.

| Marks Range  | Grade | Grade Point |
| ------------ | ----- | ----------- |
| 75 and above | A+    | 4.00        |
| 70 – 74      | A     | 4.00        |
| 65 – 69      | A-    | 3.70        |
| 60 – 64      | B+    | 3.30        |
| 55 – 59      | B     | 3.00        |
| 50 – 54      | B-    | 2.70        |
| 45 – 49      | C+    | 2.30        |
| 40 – 44      | C     | 2.00        |
| 35 – 39      | C-    | 1.70        |
| 30 – 34      | D+    | 1.30        |
| 25 – 29      | D     | 1.00        |
| 00 – 24      | E     | 0.00        |
